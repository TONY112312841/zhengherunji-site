OEM & ODM
ORDER PROCESS

zhengherunji has a professional research & development team and experienced international commerce team which have been worked in this industry for more than 15 years. We accept OEM/ODM customization according to customers' requirements.
Step 1：Requirement Communication Step 2：Quotation Step 3：Hand-make Sample Approval Step 4：Sign contract & Pay the deposit Step 5：Production Step 6：Final Random Inspection Step 7：Delivery Step 8：Balance Payment and Release Shipping Documents Step 9：After the order Step 10：Customer sharing

Requirement Communication

Customer sends us exact sample to copy; Customer sends us technical drawings to turn design into reality; Customer sends us pictures for reference; Customer sends us initial idea of a project and we discuss details together
zhengherunji will think over customersrequest and share our ideas with customer to clarify every detail to make the initial design more practical.
zhengherunji provides 2D or 3D technical drawings to customer for reference and confirmation.
Customer confirms the technical drawings.

Quotation

zhengherunji evaluate cost as per approved drawings and offer reasonable prices to customers
Communicate with customers about quotation details such as material, surface treatment, price, lead time and so on to work out a satisfied and market-oriented cost.
Customer agrees

Hand-make Sample Approval

Customer entrusts us to make samples as per approved drawings.
Customer provides comments of the samples and modify the design or technique if necessary
Customer confirms or signs the samples if it’s acceptable.

Sign contract & Pay the deposit

Customer sends us official Purchasing Order with signature and company chop and we send back Performa Invoice in return to confirm orders.
Customer pays for deposit upon signed PO/PI.

Production

zhengherunji establish product file in our database as per signed samples.
zhengherunji develop moulds and jigs for mass production.
zhengherunji produce bulk samples and compare with signed sample version or send to customer for confirmation if required.
zhengherunji keep supervising and inspecting the whole production process including incoming material, in-line production, semi-finished products and finished products.
zhengherunji timely communicate with customer and reflect the production status.

Final Random Inspection

We will advise customer 5-7 days before finishing packaging in our factory so that customer can arrange FRI if required.

Delivery

We will advise customer 5-7 days before about contact information of sea forwarder . If they don’t have ,we would quote our bottom sea freight cost with best services for a references.
If customers have their own sea forwarder ,we would start the shipment process and share with customers shipping plan.After received shipment confirmation, we would load the container and offer draft shipping documents for customers confirmation.
Confirm the content of shipping document to sea forwarder and print out original version.

Balance Payment and Release Shipping Documents

Send the copy of original shipping documents to customer for balance payment .
Express or release out original shipping documents to customer as per agreed payment terms .

After the order

Follow up with customer about order feedback on product quality and following services so that to know how to make improvement on the future new cooperation.

Customer sharing

Share with cooperated customers new design and patent products at regular intervals .
