About
Wire shelving specialist since 1999

zhengherunji Co., Ltd. was founded in 1999 in Bazhou Shengfang, Hebei, China. For 27 years we have specialized exclusively in wire shelving — for home storage, industrial display, and commercial applications. Our products reach over 80 countries, with 80% of exports going to Japan, the United States, and the European Union.

Our factory in Shengfang, Bazhou houses 220 employees and produces over 1,200 containers annually. Every process — from steel forming and robotic welding to chrome plating and powder coating — is completed in-house. We are audited and approved by ISO, NSF, BSCI, TUV, Target, and Lowe's.
