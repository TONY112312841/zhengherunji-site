About zhengherunji

Bazhou Shengfang zhengherunji Co., Ltd. is located in Hebei, China, established in 1999, has specialized in the manufacture of wire shelving for home storage and industrial display, exporting to over 80 countries, 80% to Japan, America & EU market. Our factory area is 30,000m² with 220 employees, our annual output is over 1,200pcs 40' containers. 70% of production machines are automatic and newly introduced for our new factory. All the production process including surface finishing of chrome plating and powder coating are produced in our own factory.

We are approved by American NSF organization, audited by ISO, Target, BSCI and Lowes. We are also awarded by the Chinese market with Famous Brands, such as Top 10 Brands in the Chinese Houseware Industry and Top 500 Chinese High Quality Brands. We have an in-house test room for salt spray test, coating corrosion test, loading test and dropping test etc. Besides, our products have passed the quality test by CNAS, SGS, BV and TUV. All this ensures us to obtain high reputation of stable quality and cost effectiveness in the same trade. New product design and development is operated in-house. We have our own 30 patent designs. Our target is to be the most professional factory in the wire shelving industry.

Brand Story

ZHENGHERUNJI 正合 is a brand of Bazhou Shengfang zhengherunji Co., Ltd.,

focusing on the R&D and production of home shelves and commercial shelves.


"正" Chinese Words, The shape of the words like the product pole of wire shelving in Chinese.

"合" Chinese Words, The shape of the words like the product shelf of wire shelving in Chinese.

"ZHENGHERUNJI" means beautiful place suitable for living.


"润" is also a philosophy of life, symbolizing that life will remain in the flow of the river, well-organized.

"润" Hai Na Bai Chuan, all-encompassing, is tolerant, is broad-minded;

"极" is orderliness in Chinese, is in line with nature, the normal heart,

When the two fuse, broad-minded, ordinary mentality, can let us face any environmental changes.
